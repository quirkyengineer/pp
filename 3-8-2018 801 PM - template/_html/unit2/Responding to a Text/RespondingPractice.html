Responding: Practice

Activity

Write a response to Farah Marfatia’s video post, roughly 100-150 words, addressing any of the questions posed in the “How to Respond” segment.

  1. Write the question you are addressing in the subject line of your post.
  2. Post your response to your group discussion board
  3. Respond to at least one of your peers’ posts. Don’t simply write “good job” or “I agree”--engage with their ideas by adding to what they are saying, taking their ideas in a new direction, respectfully disagreeing and saying why, etc. Your response should also be 100-150 words.

Tip:

  • Your response should add new, original ideas that are not in the source - i.e., you are not summarizing now, even though it’s based on the source.
