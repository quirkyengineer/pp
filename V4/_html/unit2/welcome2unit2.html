Welcome to Unit 2

This week, we focus on how to summarize and describe text, whether a written article, video, image, or some other published artefact. Summary is a foundational skill we will use and build on for the entire course.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this week you will be able to:

  1. summarize a text;
  2. respond to a text;
  3. apply conventions of summary-response writing; and
  4. apply strategies of concision to your writing.
