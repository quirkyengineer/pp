Sample Tables

Note: Tables can be created easily within the eCentenniail "Edit HTML File" window. For accessibility reasons, tables must include a "caption", "table heading" and "data cells". Click on the Accessibility Checker (located on the bottom right of the window) to check if your tables follow AODA standards.
